I have spent most of my career directing programs for local television. The majority of those programs where local newscasts. To give you an idea, a producer will spend hours organizing and writing all of the information, graphics, and video elements that they want for their show and put all of that information into a rundown. That rundown contains the list of stories in the order that the producer wants those stories to run, as well as the accompanying visual information for those stories, for exampe: video, or an "over-the-shoulder" graphic. They will then hand that rundown to a TV director.

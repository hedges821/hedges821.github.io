I have spent most of my career directing programs for local television. Mostly, that consisted of local newscasts. To give you an idea, a producer will spend hours organizing and writing all of the information, graphics, and video elements that they want for their show into a rundown. That rundown contains the list of stories in the order that the producer wants those stories to run, and the accompanying visual information for those stories, such as video, or an "over-the-shoulder" graphic. They will then hand that rundown to a TV director.
